The Legend Of SICARUS

Sicarus was born from late night jam sessions in a basement in Okotoks Alberta and a dream to be heard around the world. The crew of four consists of the lead singer and guitarist Kiefer, The Bassist Mitch, Pianist Caleb, and Powerhouse Drummer Vlad. The group have been friends for years and all made the commitment to drop studies and focus on music after the band started gaining traction. After just 3 years of starting “Sicarus” is well known around Canada and slowly spreading further!
